Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 12, 2026

Welcome to MiniGames! This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a safe gaming experience.

1. Information We Collect

MiniGames is designed with your privacy in mind. We want to be transparent about what information we collect:

We DO NOT collect any personal information.
  • No Personal Data: We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, phone number, or location.
  • No Account Required: You can play all games without creating an account or providing any personal details.
  • Local Storage Only: Game scores and progress are stored locally on your device using browser localStorage. This data never leaves your device.

2. How We Use Information

Since we don't collect any personal information, there is nothing to use, share, or sell. The only data stored is:

  • Game Scores: Your best scores are saved locally on your device to track your progress.
  • Game Preferences: Any game settings or preferences are stored locally.

This data is stored using your device's local storage and is never transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

3. Third-Party Services

MiniGames does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics tools, or advertising networks. We do not use:

  • Analytics services (like Google Analytics)
  • Advertising networks
  • Social media integrations
  • Crash reporting tools
  • Any other third-party SDKs or services

4. Data Security

Since all game data is stored locally on your device and we don't collect or transmit any information, your data is as secure as your device. We recommend:

  • Keeping your device's operating system updated
  • Using device security features like screen locks
  • Being cautious about installing apps from unknown sources

5. Children's Privacy

MiniGames is suitable for all ages. Since we don't collect any personal information, there are no special considerations for children's privacy. Parents can feel confident that their children's information is not being collected or shared.

6. Permissions

MiniGames requires minimal permissions to function:

  • Internet Access: Required to load game assets (HTML/CSS/JavaScript files) from the app bundle.
  • No Other Permissions: We do not request access to your camera, microphone, contacts, location, or any other sensitive device features.

7. Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete it at any time by:

  • Clearing the app's cache and data through your device settings
  • Uninstalling and reinstalling the app
  • Using your device's storage management tools

Note: Deleting this data will reset all your game scores and progress.

8. Changes to Games

The app contains 8 offline games:

  • PULSE - Timing and rhythm game
  • TRACE - Memory and pattern game
  • GRID - Logic puzzle game
  • VOID - Sequence survival game
  • CIPHER - Cryptography puzzle game
  • RUN - Endless runner game
  • LIMBO - Platform adventure game
  • BADLAND - Flying obstacle game

All games run entirely offline and do not require an internet connection after initial installation.

9. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

If we make any material changes to how we handle user information, we will notify users through the app or by other means.

10. Your Rights

Since we don't collect any personal information, there is no data for you to access, modify, or delete from our servers. However, you have complete control over the local data stored on your device.

11. International Users

MiniGames can be used worldwide. Since we don't collect or transfer any personal data, there are no international data transfer concerns. All data remains on your local device.

12. No Advertising

MiniGames is completely ad-free. We do not display advertisements, and we do not share any information with advertising networks.

13. Consent

By using MiniGames, you consent to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use the app.
